About Breast Cancer

Breast cancer is a type of cancer that develops in breast cells. It occurs when cells in the breast grow and divide uncontrollably, creating a mass of tissue known as a tumor. Detecting it early increases the chances of successful treatment.

Our tool aims to support healthcare professionals by providing a quick and reliable analysis to aid in the early detection of breast cancer.
